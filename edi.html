Event coordination and outreach

I have wrote articles sharing my experience as a person in STEM. In 2024 I was part of the organising commitee of WoNDRS - Conference for Women and Non-Binary Doctoral Researchers, that took place at the University of Liverpool. I have also joined as a member of the EDI commitee at the department of Mathematical Sciences.

WoNDRS Conference

The WoNDRS event focused on the outstanding work lead by women and non-binary people across all STEM subjects, encouraging students to engage in networking and allowing space for women and other gender minorities to be celebrated for their achievements.

EDI

I am a current member of the EDI group at the University of Liverpool.

Journal Club organiser

I have been one of the organisers of the Journal Club at the Maths Department of the Univeristy of Liverpool during my PhD. Weekly speakers are invited to share their research and discuss ideas and current aiXiv papers.
